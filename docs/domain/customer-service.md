# Customer Service Domain

Support and grievance resolution solutions for customers' shopping with brands.

## 1. Support Ticket
*   Customers submit support requests related to brand products directly on the application.
*   Brand staff receive, respond, and update the processing status of the ticket (Processing, Resolved, Closed).

## 2. Return and Exchange Request
*   A standardized process allowing users to register for returning or exchanging products purchased from the Brand through the application interface, integrated with the brand's warranty policy.
