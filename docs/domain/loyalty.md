# Loyalty Domain

The Loyalty system helps connect and increase engagement between fashion brands and their customers.

## 1. Loyalty Point Transactions
*   **Earning points**: Users accumulate points when shopping at the Brand (scanning receipt codes, linking shopping accounts) or participating in surveys and voting for prototypes in the Digital Sample Lab.
*   **Spending points**: Use accumulated points to redeem exclusive brand gifts and discount codes.

## 2. Loyalty Tiers
Brands configure different membership tiers based on accumulated points (e.g., Bronze, Silver, Gold, Diamond) to apply automatic discount promotions or special care privileges.

## 3. Brand Benefit
Financial or non-financial benefits such as: free shipping, birthday gifts, priority returns, early fitting of new collections.
