# Wardrobe Domain

Digital wardrobe management is the core of the B2C experience, allowing virtualization of physical wardrobes.

## 1. Wardrobe Item
Each clothing item in the system is managed by the following attributes:
*   **ID**: Unique identifier.
*   **Media**: URLs of the originally uploaded image and the background-removed processed image.
*   **Category**: Top, Bottom, Dress, Shoes, Accessory.
*   **Properties**: Color, Material, Season, Style.
*   **Decay State**: Old/new status of the item over time based on usage (Garment Lifecycle Decay).

## 2. Closet Initialization Catalog
A quick wardrobe initialization process for new users by selecting basic clothing items from the system's Catalog instead of having to take pictures and upload each of their own items.
