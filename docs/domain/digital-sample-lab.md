# Digital Sample Lab Domain

Digital Sample Lab is a strategic B2B solution helping brands test designs before moving into mass production.

## 1. Digital Sample
*   Sample products are created in the form of high-quality design images or 3D models.
*   Contains information about design inspiration, proposed materials, and expected size charts.

## 2. Sample Outfit Trial
Users can virtually try on this sample design in their personal digital wardrobe or on a virtual body via AI assistance to see style compatibility.

## 3. Sample Vote & Feedback
*   **Vote**: Users vote to agree/disagree with production or rate their preference from 1-5 stars.
*   **Feedback**: Users leave detailed opinions on form, shape, color, or material.

## 4. Insight Dashboard & Data Privacy
*   **Insight Dashboard**: Provides the Brand with statistical charts of votes, age demographics of interested users, and highlighted feedback.
*   **Data Privacy**: Ensures all statistical data is anonymized, not disclosing users' personal information to the brand.
